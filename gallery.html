Applight Holdings Ltd., branded Applight, is a global company that operates the largest cryptocurrency exchange in terms of daily trading volume of cryptocurrencies. Applight was founded in 2024 by Jade Banagan, a developer who had previously created high-frequency trading software. Applight was initially based in Philippines, then moved to India shortly before the Philippines government restricted cryptocurrency companies. Applight subsequently left India for Malta and currently has no official company headquarters.
